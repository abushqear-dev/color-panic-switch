# Color Panic Switch

A fast-paced color-matching arcade game built with Godot Engine. Test your reflexes as colored squares fall from the sky—catch them with the right color to score, but watch out for mistakes!

## Description

In **Color Panic Switch**, players control a catcher at the bottom of the screen that can switch between three colors: red, green, and blue. Colored squares spawn randomly at the top and fall downward at increasing speeds. Match the catcher's color to the falling square to earn points and play a satisfying sound. Mismatch, and you'll accumulate mistakes. Reach 10 mistakes, and the game restarts!

The game gets progressively harder as fall speeds accelerate, challenging your timing and color-switching skills.

## Features

- **Simple Controls**: Switch colors with arrow keys (or A and D) for quick reactions.
- **Increasing Difficulty**: Fall speed ramps up over time for endless challenge.
- **Audio Feedback**: Distinct sounds for matches and misses.
- **Responsive Design**: Adapts to different screen sizes.
- **Score Tracking**: Keep track of your points and mistakes in real-time.

## How to Play

1. Use the left and right arrow keys (or A and D) to change the catcher's color.
2. Match the color of falling squares to score points.
3. Avoid letting mismatched squares reach the bottom (counts as a mistake).
4. Survive as long as possible without reaching 10 mistakes!
5. The game restarts automatically after 10 mistakes.

## Controls

- **Left Arrow (or A)**: Switch catcher color to the previous color (red → blue → green → red).
- **Right Arrow (or D)**: Switch catcher color to the next color (red → green → blue → red).
